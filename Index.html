Raw Material

The starting material is made from biopolymer and natural plant fibers (BAMBOO). It is 100% Biodegradable and certified AS5810 Home Compostable, making the bottle an environment-promoting product inside the university campus.

There is no extra investment in new equipment or machinery needed. Only simple and minor adjustments are required to accommodate these raw materials.
